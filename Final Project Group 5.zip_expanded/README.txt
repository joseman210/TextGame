Run from TextAdventure Class
Your player is a rutgers student that needs to gather certain items in order to graduate.
The items have either a positive or negative numerical value. 
The player gets asked a question and based on his response he can choose to accept or decline the item
At the end of the game, the value of the items in the inventory is added up and if score is equal to or greater than 5.5 the player graduates, otherwise
they fail and the game restarts.
